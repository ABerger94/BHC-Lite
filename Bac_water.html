Bacteriostatic Water (BAC Water) is sterile water containing 0.9% benzyl alcohol as a bacteriostatic preservative. It is commonly used in research settings to reconstitute lyophilized peptides and compounds.

Each 20mL multi-dose vial is sealed and labeled for research use only. Not for human or veterinary injection or consumption.

Volume: 20mL per vial
Preservative: 0.9% Benzyl Alcohol
Container: Sterile glass vial, tamper-evident
Application: Peptide reconstitution (RUO only)
Storage: Store at 15–25°C, protect from light

Can I inject this?
No. This product is strictly for laboratory research use only (RUO) and is not intended for injection or medical applications.

What’s the shelf life?
Unopened: 18–24 months. Once accessed, use within 28 days if stored properly.

What’s the difference between sterile and bacteriostatic water?
Bacteriostatic water contains a preservative (benzyl alcohol), allowing it to be used for multiple withdrawals in a sterile research environment.
