Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill orders

We use secure third-party platforms for payment processing and encrypted data handling. Access to personal information is limited to authorized personnel only.

We may use cookies to personalize your experience and analyze website usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may share necessary information with trusted vendors (e.g., shipping, payment processors) to complete your transaction. We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest revision date will be noted here. Continued use of our site constitutes acceptance of any changes.
